Technical analysis concepts for your ForgeAI positions.
SUPPORT & RESISTANCE
Price levels where buying/selling historically reverses. CLOV has strong support at $1.93 (52wk low). UNH add zone $255–265.
RSI — RELATIVE STRENGTH INDEX
Momentum oscillator 0–100. Below 30 = oversold (potential buy). Above 70 = overbought (potential sell). RSI below 30 on CLOV or UNH after a selloff = strong entry signal.
MOVING AVERAGES (EMA)
EMA20 = short trend. EMA50 = medium. EMA200 = long. Price above all three = strong uptrend. Golden cross (EMA50 > EMA200) = major bullish signal.
BOLLINGER BANDS
Bands 2 standard deviations from mean. Price touching lower band = oversold bounce. Band squeeze (bands narrowing) = volatility breakout incoming.
VOLUME ANALYSIS
Volume confirms price moves. Rising price + rising volume = sustainable. Rising price + falling volume = weak, may reverse. For HMBL: 5x normal volume = first alert trigger.
